Description for 7841: Video Tape Rental  Division I: Services  |  Major Group 78: Motion Pictures  | Industry Group 784: Video Tape Rental  7841 Video Tape Rental  Establishments primarily engaged in renting recorded video tapes and disks to the general public for personal or household use. Establishments primarily engaged in renting video recorders and players are classified in Industry 7359. Establishments primarily engaged in selling recorded video tapes and disks to the general public are classified in Retail Trade, Industry 5735, and those engaged in the wholesale distribution of recorded video tapes and disks are classified in Industry 7822.
