Description for 5531: Auto and Home Supply Stores  Division G: Retail Trade  |  Major Group 55: Automotive Dealers And Gasoline Service Stations  | Industry Group 553: Auto And Home Supply Stores  5531 Auto and Home Supply Stores  Establishments primarily engaged in the retail sale of new automobile tires, batteries, and other automobile parts and accessories. Such establishments frequently sell a substantial amount of home appliances, radios, and television sets. Establishments dealing primarily in used parts are classified in Wholesale Trade, Industry 5015. Establishments primarily engaged in both selling and installing such automotive parts as transmissions, mufflers, brake linings, and glass are classified in Services, Industry Group 753.
