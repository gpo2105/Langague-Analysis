Description for 6798: Real Estate Investment Trusts  Division H: Finance, Insurance, And Real Estate  |  Major Group 67: Holding And Other Investment Offices  | Industry Group 679: Miscellaneous Investing  6798 Real Estate Investment Trusts  Establishments primarily engaged in closed-end investments in real estate or related mortgage assets operating so that they could meet the requirements of the Real Estate Investment Trust Act of 1960 as amended. This act exempts trusts from corporate income and capital gains taxation, provided they invest primarily in specified assets, pay out most of their income to shareholders, and meet certain requirements regarding the dispersion of trust ownership.
