Description for 4833: Television Broadcasting Stations  Division E: Transportation, Communications, Electric, Gas, And Sanitary Services  |  Major Group 48: Communications  | Industry Group 483: Radio And Television Broadcasting Stations  4833 Television Broadcasting Stations  Establishments primarily engaged in broadcasting visual programs by television to the public, except cable and other pay television services. Included in this industry are commercial, religious, educational, and other television stations. Also included here are establishments primarily engaged in television broadcasting and which produce taped television program materials. Separate establishments primarily engaged in producing taped television program materials are classified in Services, Industry 7812. Establishments primarily engaged in furnishing cable and other pay television services are classified in Industry 4841.  Television broadcasting stations
