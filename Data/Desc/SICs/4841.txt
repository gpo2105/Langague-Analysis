Description for 4841: Cable and Other Pay Television Services  Division E: Transportation, Communications, Electric, Gas, And Sanitary Services  |  Major Group 48: Communications  | Industry Group 484: Cable And Other Pay Television Services  4841 Cable and Other Pay Television Services  Establishments primarily engaged in the dissemination of visual and textual television programs, on a subscription or fee basis. Included in this industry are establishments which are primarily engaged in cablecasting and which also produce taped program materials. Separate establishments primarily engaged in producing taped television or motion picture program materials are classified in Services, Industry 7812.  Cable television services  Closed circuit television services  Direct broadcast satellite (DBS) services  Multipoint distribution systems (MDS) services  Satellite master antenna systems (SMATV) services  Subscription television services
