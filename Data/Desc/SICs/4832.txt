Description for 4832: Radio Broadcasting Stations  Division E: Transportation, Communications, Electric, Gas, And Sanitary Services  |  Major Group 48: Communications  | Industry Group 482: Telegraph And Other Message Communications  4832 Radio Broadcasting Stations  Establishments primarily engaged in broadcasting aural programs by radio to the public. Included in this industry are commercial, religious, educational, and other radio stations. Also included here are establishments primarily engaged in radio broadcasting and which produce radio program materials. Separate establishments primarily engaged in producing radio program materials are classified in Services, Industry 7922.  Radio broadcasting stations
